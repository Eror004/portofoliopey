THE CHALLENGE

Nova Tech approached us with a need to establish a strong visual presence in the competitive tech market. They required a brand identity that would communicate innovation, reliability, and forward-thinking approach while remaining approachable to their diverse customer base.

THE PROCESS

Our design process began with extensive research into the tech industry landscape and Nova's target audience. We conducted stakeholder interviews, competitive analysis, and brand workshops to understand the core values and differentiators. Through iterative design explorations, we developed a visual system that balances technological sophistication with human warmth.

THE SOLUTION

The final brand identity features a bold, geometric logomark that represents connectivity and innovation. The color palette combines deep tech blues with vibrant accent colors, while the custom typography system ensures brand consistency across all touchpoints. We delivered a comprehensive brand guidelines document and asset library.
